Give your pet peace of mind, get a snapshot into their day.

PetBot is an amazing robotic toy that allows you to have a full on remote conversation with your pet! It even lets you watch from the camera, and if you really like what Fluffy is up to, you can press a button on your phone and the Petbot shoots a treat out it’s mouth.

How does it work?

  • Account
  • Connecting
  • Reboting
  • Pairing
  • Redirecting

Create an Account

After creating an account press the pairing button to enter the Pairing Options page. (to ensure a smooth connection, please pair the device five minutes after the power button is turned on.)

Connect the Device

Connect the device using the Scanning or Bluetooth Options. If connecting via Bluetooth, make sure the Bluetooth function on your device is enabled.

Rebooting Device

Click the power button on the bottom of your Pet Robot to automatically transfer the device number and access code to your app.
If the device will not connect automatically, press the Power Button once and the root will announce device number. After a few seconds the information should fill itself automatically.

Click PAIRING.

The app will bring up a PAIRING SUCCSESSFUL! screen when the app is paired.

Back to Home Page.

Click the I KNOW button. The app will return you to the device home page.
When the device is successfully connected the app will open the main Device page. During this time, please connect your WIFI.
